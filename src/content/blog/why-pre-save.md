## Understanding Spotify Pre-Save Campaigns

Before we delve into the nitty-gritty of setting up and promoting pre-save campaigns, let&#8217;s first define what a pre-save campaign is and why it&#8217;s important for artists.

### What is A Pre-Save Campaign?

A pre-save campaign is a music marketing tool used during the pre-release phase; it allows fans to save upcoming music releases to their music library or playlists before they are officially released.

It&#8217;s the modern day version of a &#8220;pre-order&#8221;.

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img data-recalc-dims="1" loading="lazy" decoding="async" width="800" height="419" src="https://i0.wp.com/oddlysimpl.xyz/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/Website-Graphics-11.png?resize=800%2C419&#038;ssl=1" alt="" class="wp-image-230" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/oddlysimpl.xyz/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/Website-Graphics-11.png?resize=1024%2C536&amp;ssl=1 1024w, https://i0.wp.com/oddlysimpl.xyz/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/Website-Graphics-11.png?resize=300%2C157&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i0.wp.com/oddlysimpl.xyz/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/Website-Graphics-11.png?resize=768%2C402&amp;ssl=1 768w, https://i0.wp.com/oddlysimpl.xyz/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/Website-Graphics-11.png?w=1200&amp;ssl=1 1200w" sizes="(max-width: 800px) 100vw, 800px" /></figure>

Pre-save campaigns serve as a way for artists to gather necessary information, such as email addresses (or phone numbers if using Laylo), which can be used for future marketing efforts.

One of the great things about pre-save campaigns is that they are simple tasks for fans to complete.

With just a few clicks, fans can save new releases to their music library.

### The Importance of Pre-Save Campaigns for Artists

Pre-save campaigns provide artists with valuable data, such as audience demographics, an understanding of demand behind a song, or even seeing what artists their fanbase listens to.

In a highly competitive music industry, pre-save campaigns also help artists stand out and attract attention from A&amp;Rs at record labels and management companies.

Essentially, pre-saves are a great way of gauging demand for a song.

### What&#8217;s a pre-save campaign?

A pre-save campaign on Spotify allows fans to save an upcoming release from an artist to their library or playlists before it&#8217;s officially released. 

### What are the benefits of pre-save on Spotify?

Pre-save campaigns are important because they can help an artist capture first party fan data. Additionally, a successful pre-save campaign can also help with having leverage when talking to A&amp;Rs or your distributor/record label to secure playlist placements for your forthcoming release, whether it be a single, EP or album.

### What is the point of pre saving music?

The point of pre-saving music is to allow fans to save an upcoming release from an artist to their library or playlists before its official release. This helps artists capture first-party fan data and can also be used as leverage when securing playlist placements for the release.

### How do you run a successful pre save campaign?

To create a successful pre-save campaign for your music release on Spotify, follow these steps:

- **Set a clear goal** for your campaign, whether it&#8217;s to increase first day streams, gather email addresses, or build hype for your release.

- **Offer an exclusive incentive **for fans to pre-save your music, such as early access, exclusive content, or merchandise giveaways.

- **Utilize social media **and email marketing to promote your pre-save campaign. Craft attention-grabbing captions and visuals, and encourage fans to share and spread the word.

- **Make the pre-save process simple** and easy for fans

Remember, creating a successful pre-save campaign is an iterative process.

Analyze campaign metrics, track engagement, and adapt your strategies to continually improve and refine your campaign.

### What are some best practices for promoting my pre-save campaign?

To effectively promote your pre-save campaign, consider these best practices:

- **Start early;** promoting your campaign at least 2-3 weeks before the release date to build anticipation and engagement.

- **Create engaging content** on social media; use the simpl. TikTok content framework for content ideas

- **Have an incentive;** exclusive content, early access, or behind-the-scenes footage to fans who pre-save your music, creating a sense of exclusivity and excitement.

By following these best practices, you can maximize engagement, visibility, and fan participation in your pre-save campaign.

### What metrics should I track to measure the success of my pre-save campaign?

To measure the success of your pre-save campaign, track the following metrics:

- **Number of pre-saves:** Monitor the number of users who have pre-saved your release, as this indicates fan engagement and interest.

- **Conversion rate:** Calculate the percentage of users who pre-save after visiting your landing page, providing insights into your campaign&#8217;s effectiveness.

- **Social media engagement: **Keep track of likes, shares, and comments on your social media posts related to your pre-save campaign, as this indicates audience engagement.

- **Streaming metrics:** After release day, track streams, playlist adds, and saves on platforms like Spotify and Apple Music to assess the campaign&#8217;s impact on fan engagement.

By monitoring these metrics, you can gauge the success of your campaign, identify areas for improvement, and make data-driven decisions for future marketing efforts.
