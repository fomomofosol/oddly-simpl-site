## What videos to use for music marketing

A main content piece such as a music video or performance video is a perfect start for your marketing efforts.

We took the entirety of their music videos, resized them into vertical videos, and ran 8-12 creatives for every ad set.

Rather than guessing what part of the song resonated with fans and led to conversions, we let the data speak for itself. We wanted fans to tell us what they liked, so that we could continue to supply it.

This brought in solid results across the board, and even solid engagement on the ads themselves.
